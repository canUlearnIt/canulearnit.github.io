About us


Our company - Happy Used Auto was founded in 1993. It was a small shop on the outskirts of the city. Our customers were mostly friends, family and neighbours. Since then we have grown into a big car dealer. We provide the best service in town! We make sure that all our customers are happy. We sell different types of cars at various prices. The selection of cars is huge so there is a car for any taste. Please visit us at 232 Bloor st. or fill out our online form and we will find the car you need.
